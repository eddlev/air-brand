# Using the AIR Brand

The **framework code** in VM4AI's repositories is licensed under **Apache-2.0**. The **brand** is separate: the **AIR** and **VM4AI** names, the wordmark, and the **dot-in-frame mark** are reserved and are *not* granted by the code license. These terms explain what's welcome and what isn't.

## The "Made with AIR" stamp

The stamp signals that a project was genuinely built with AIR.

**Welcome**
- Add it to projects, repos, or sites actually built with AIR.
- Link it to `https://vm4ai.com`.
- Use any provided variant (pill or seal, clean or period) in SVG or PNG, unmodified.

**Please don't**
- Use it on projects not built with AIR.
- Recolor it, change its proportions, or restyle the ember dot.

Markdown badge:

```markdown
[![Made with AIR](made-with-air/made-with-air.svg)](https://vm4ai.com)
```

## The AIR name and mark

**Welcome**
- Refer to AIR and VM4AI by name in articles, posts, talks, tutorials, and reviews.
- Show the unmodified logo when crediting, linking to, or writing about AIR.
- Quote the brand book and link back to the source.

**Please don't**
- Imply endorsement, sponsorship, affiliation, or an official partnership that doesn't exist.
- Alter the mark — its color, proportions, frame, or the ember dot.
- Use the AIR or VM4AI names (or a confusingly similar name) in your own product name, logo, company name, or domain.
- Present modified assets as the official brand.

## In short

Use AIR's name and mark to point *to* AIR, not to brand something *as* AIR. When in doubt, ask first.
